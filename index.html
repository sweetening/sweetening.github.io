About Me

After 17 years in the service industry, amid the Coronavirus pandemic in Spring 2020, I decided to pivot my career and pursue web development by joining Le Wagon Berlin for a 9 week intensive bootcamp. During that time I built my first apps and in the process discovered my passion for creating digital products, nurtured my creativity in web design, and expanded my knowledge of programming languages.


I am presently seeking opportunities at a growing startup with a strong commitment to social causes, particularly those that create opportunities for trans and nonbinary people and women globally.
